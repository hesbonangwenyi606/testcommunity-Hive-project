Our Story

Founded in 2020, Test Hive began as a small group of QA professionals meeting monthly in Nairobi to share knowledge and challenges.

What started as casual meetups has grown into Kenya's premier quality assurance community with over 500 active members across the country.

We're proud to have helped hundreds of testers improve their skills, find mentors, and advance their careers in QA.
